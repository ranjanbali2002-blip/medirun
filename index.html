Why choose us

Healthcare delivery,
done right

We're not just another courier. MediRun is built specifically for the Indian pharmacy ecosystem — with real prescription verification, licensed pharmacies, and safety-first delivery.

Licensed pharmacies only
Every pharmacy on MediRun holds a valid Drug License under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940. No grey-market medicines, no substitution without consent.
Prescription-verified orders
Upload your prescription directly in the app. A qualified pharmacist reviews it before dispatching Schedule H & H1 medicines. No cutting corners.
Real-time GPS tracking
Know your rider's exact location on a live Leaflet map — updated every 5 seconds. No fake status updates, no "rider not found."
Delivery OTP security
A unique 4-digit code confirms every handover — just like Rapido and Swiggy. Your medicines will never end up with the wrong person.

List your pharmacy on MediRun and start receiving delivery orders within 24 hours. We handle riders, the app, and payment settlement — you focus on dispensing medicines correctly. Zero setup cost. Commission only on completed orders. Suitable for independent chemists across Punjab.
